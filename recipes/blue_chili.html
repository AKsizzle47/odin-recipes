Blue chili

Description

Blue chili is a great dish because it's healthy, delicious, and... it's BLUE!

Ingredients

  • ground turkey
  • yellow onion
  • canned diced tomatoes
  • spices
  • blue dye

Steps

  1. cook the ground turkey in a pan
  2. drain out the fat from the ground turkey
  3. chop up the onion
  4. add the turkey, onion, tomatoes, and spices to a slow cooker
  5. cook on high heat for 3 hours
  6. add the blue dye
  7. stir it up
  8. enjoy!
