Forbidden chili

Description

This chili is disallowed. Do not make it. Do not eat it.

Ingredients

  • ground turkey
  • yellow onion
  • canned diced tomatoes
  • spices
  • secrets
  • fear

Steps

  1. cook the ground turkey in a pan
  2. drain out the fat from the ground turkey
  3. chop up the onion
  4. add the turkey, onion, tomatoes, and spices to a slow cooker
  5. cook on high heat for 3 hours
  6. if you made it this far, you are now a fugitive
  7. RUN
